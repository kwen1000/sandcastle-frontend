Productivity timer

Start by thinking of a task that needs to be done. Then, work on that task for 25 minutes. After 25 minutes, take a break for 5 minutes. And repeat. I made this for myself, but you are more than welcome to use it.

Each timer's interval is one minute to limit battery drainage compared to an interval of one second. A notification will popup (if your browser supports notifications) when a timer is done.
